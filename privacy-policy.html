Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Overview

Attune ("the App") is a music player that retunes audio to 432 Hz. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data Collection

Attune does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your music, playlists, favorites, and settings are stored locally on your device and never leave it.

Audio Files

The App accesses audio files on your device solely for playback purposes. Audio files are not uploaded, shared, or transmitted to any server.

Advertising

The free version of the App may display ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases processed through Google Play. Payment information is handled entirely by Google and is not accessible to us.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — ad serving (free version only)
  • RevenueCat — purchase management (anonymous user IDs only)

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at attune@fabio.dev.
